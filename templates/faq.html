General

What is Exclusive Aim?
Exclusive Aim is a highly advanced computer vision (CV) based assistance software written primarily in C++. It uses real-time AI to detect targets and provide smooth, human-like aim assistance without ever injecting code into your game.
Is it safe to use?
Yes. Because we use Computer Vision and external hardware injection (Titan Two/Macku), the software is completely external. It does not read or write game memory, making it virtually undetectable to standard anti-cheats (Ricochet, Vanguard, BattlEye, EAC).

Hardware

Do I need specific hardware?
For Console or Dual-PC setups, a Capture Card is required to feed the video to the AI. For output, we support Titan Two and Macku devices for 100% undetectable hardware-level spoofing.
What is the "Macku" device?
Macku is our proprietary hardware injection solution. It supports 1000Hz polling, ultra-smooth tracking, anti-recoil, and rapid fire. It is a premium alternative to the Titan Two, built specifically for high-performance CV aiming.
Is a Capture Card required for Single PC?
No. Single PC users can use direct window capture. However, using a Titan Two or Macku is still highly recommended for Single PC to avoid direct software mouse manipulation, which is safer.
