General

What is Exclusive Aim?
Exclusive Aim is a professional gaming enhancement suite powered by neural computer vision (CV). It uses real-time AI to assist with target tracking and precision movement, providing a more fluid and efficient gaming experience without modified game files.
Is it safe to use?
Yes. Because we use Computer Vision and external hardware injection (Titan Two/Macku), the software is completely external. It does not read or write game memory, making it virtually undetectable to standard anti-cheats (Ricochet, Vanguard, BattlEye, EAC).

Hardware

Do I need specific hardware?
For Console or Dual-PC setups, a Capture Card is used to process the video feed. For output, we support Titan Two and Macku devices for professional-grade hardware-level input isolation.
What is the "Macku" device?
Macku is a hardware injection solution that supports 1000Hz polling, ultra-smooth tracking, anti-recoil, and rapid fire. It is an alternative to the Titan Two, built for high-performance CV aiming. (Note: For console use, Macku always requires a XIM).
Is a Capture Card required for Single PC?
No. Single PC users can utilize direct secure capture. However, using a Titan Two or Macku is still recommended to maintain complete hardware isolation, which is the gold standard for secure implementation.

Console Usage

Yes! It works on all consoles. For PlayStation 5 specifically, a bypass device like the XIM Matrix, FCG2, or P5Mate is required to handle the console's controller authentication.

Absolutely. We have full support for XIM + Titan Two or XIM + Macku combos. For console usage, Macku always requires a XIM to function correctly. This allows you to combine the best movement of a XIM with our advanced AI target tracking.
