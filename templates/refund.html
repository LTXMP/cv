Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Exclusive Aim, we strive to provide the most advanced AI precision software. Due to the digital nature of our products, please read our refund policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. All Sales are Final

Digital software licenses are considered "used" upon delivery of the license key or activation of the account. Therefore, we do not offer general refunds or credits once a purchase is completed.

2. Technical Failures

Refunds may only be considered in the specific event of a persistent, non-resolvable technical failure that prevents the software from functioning entirely on your hardware, provided that:

  • You contacted our support team via Discord within 24 hours of purchase.
  • Our technical team has attempted to troubleshoot the issue.
  • The failure is not caused by third-party software (e.g. Antivirus, Firewalls, or incompatible Operating Systems).

3. License Misuse & Bans

No refunds will ever be issued for accounts that have been suspended due to violations of our Terms of Service, including license sharing, reverse engineering, or usage in prohibited environments.

4. Change of Mind

We do not offer refunds for "change of mind" or if you find you no longer need the software after purchase.

5. Subscription Cancellations

For recurring subscriptions, you may cancel at any time to prevent future billing. However, no partial refunds will be issued for the remaining time in your current billing cycle.
